Information you give us. When you use our forms, booking links, calculators, or job application, we collect what you submit — such as your name, email address, phone number, company or trade, messages you type, calculator inputs, and, for job applicants, your resume and application answers.

Information collected automatically. We use information to: respond to your inquiries and provide requested services; contact you about your inquiry by phone, text/SMS, and email (see Section 5); operate, personalize, secure, and improve the Site; operate automated and AI-assisted communications; evaluate job applications; perform analytics and measure marketing; comply with law; and protect our rights and the rights of the Company's parent and affiliated entities.

Please do not send us sensitive personal information (such as government identifiers, financial account numbers, or health information) through website messaging.

By providing your phone number or email through the Site, you expressly consent to receive marketing and service communications from us and our service providers — including calls and text/SMS messages sent using automated technology or AI — at the number or address you provide.
